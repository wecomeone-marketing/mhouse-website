## 2. The story

### The Story Behind M House

M House is named in honour of our mother, Mette.

The property was originally intended to become her home. However, before she had the
opportunity to move in, she sadly passed away. Faced with the question of what to do next,
we chose not to let the project become simply another property. Instead, we decided to
create a place that reflected the things she loved most: people, conversation, laughter,
hospitality and bringing others together.

M House was born from the idea of creating a house she would have loved. A place where
strangers become friends, where locals and travellers connect, and where life's simple
pleasures are celebrated.

The name serves as a daily reminder that the most meaningful moments are often the simplest
ones: sharing a meal, enjoying a drink with friends, meeting someone new, taking a swim on a
warm afternoon or simply being present with the people around you.

### Vision

At its heart, M House is about human connection.

In a world that often feels increasingly disconnected, we wanted to create a place where
people naturally come together. A place where conversations happen easily, where strangers
become friends, where locals meet travellers and where memorable moments are shared.

Whether someone comes to stay, swim, work, celebrate, attend one of our gatherings or simply
enjoy aperitivo by the pool, our goal is the same: to create a welcoming house filled with
real spirit, genuine hospitality and meaningful human connection.

## 4. Events

M House offers a unique venue in the heart of Larnaka's old town. Combining a Mediterranean
courtyard, saltwater pool, lounge areas and boutique hospitality, the property provides a
relaxed and intimate setting for both private and corporate events. Whether it is a
celebration, networking event, product launch or wellness gathering, we work closely with
each client to create a memorable experience.

**Perfect for:** product launches · fashion events · brand activations · networking events ·
corporate retreats · team building · workshops · private dinners · cocktail parties ·
birthday celebrations · engagement parties · civil ceremonies · wedding celebrations ·
wellness events · yoga events.

**Maximum capacity:** approximately 150 guests depending on setup.

### Exclusive venue hire
Exclusive use of M House is available for a minimum of 5 hours.
- Minimum spend: €800 (depending on date and event requirements)
- If the minimum spend is not reached, the difference is charged as the venue hire fee.

### Open bar packages
Includes beer, wine and soft drinks. Minimum booking 40 guests (below 40, pay per use).
- 2 hours — €27 per person
- 3 hours — €34 per person
- 4 hours — €39 per person

### Catering
Can be added to any event, and may also be ordered independently.

**Sushi selection** (minimum 40 guests). Freshly prepared, served as individual pieces.
Suggested 10 to 15 pieces per person.
- Rolls & maki — €0.95 each (incl. VAT)
- Nigiri — €1.40 each (incl. VAT)
- Varieties: California salmon, California tuna, California wakame (vegan), spicy smoked
  trout roll, creamy crab roll, creamy duck roll, shrimp roll, salmon maki, smoked trout
  maki, wakame maki (vegan), salmon nigiri, ebi shrimp nigiri.

**Canapés** (minimum 40 guests). Bespoke menu tailored to each event. Suggested 8 to 12
pieces per person. Priced individually based on menu and guest numbers.

**BBQ buffet** (minimum 20 guests). Relaxed Mediterranean BBQ: traditional souvla, fresh
salads, homemade dips, fresh bread. €17 per person.

**Catering terms:** selections confirmed no later than 7 days before the event · 50% deposit
on booking · balance due 48 hours prior.

## 5. Gatherings

One of the key objectives of M House is to create opportunities for locals and visitors to
connect through food, culture, wellness and shared experiences.

Unless otherwise stated, all gatherings are **by advance booking only**.

| Gathering | Duration | Max | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Yaya's Corner | 2 to 3 hours | 12 | €50 pp | Wine, beer, water and snacks |
| Breakfast & Swim | approx. 2 hours | 20 | €25 pp | Pool access and traditional breakfast |
| Sunrise Yoga | approx. 1 hour | 12 | €20 pp | Pool access |
| Water Yoga | approx. 1 hour | 12 | €20 pp | Pool access |
| Aperitivo | daily, 17:00 to 19:00 | — | — | Complimentary aperitivo bites |

- **Yaya's Corner** — Experience Cyprus through the eyes of local Cypriot women as they share seasonal recipes, stories and traditions passed down through generations.
- **Breakfast & Swim** — Start the day with a refreshing swim followed by a traditional Cypriot breakfast known as Boukoma.
- **Sunrise Yoga** — Start the morning with a guided yoga session before enjoying access to the pool.
- **Water Yoga** — A refreshing yoga experience combining movement and mindfulness in and around the pool.
- **Aperitivo at M House** — Inspired by the Italian tradition of gathering before dinner, our daily social ritual. Guests and visitors meet by the pool, enjoy a drink and receive complimentary aperitivo bites while watching the afternoon turn into evening. Open to guests and visitors, walk-ins welcome, reservations recommended.

Additional seasonal gatherings and experiences will be introduced throughout the year.

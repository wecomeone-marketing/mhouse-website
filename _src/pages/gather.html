Gather

Ways to come together

The house keeps a gentle rhythm. Mornings in the water, afternoons in the shade, evenings by the pool. These are the moments we set aside for each other.

The rhythm of the house

Small rituals,
shared often.

One of the key objectives of M House is to create opportunities for locals and visitors to connect through food, culture, wellness and shared experiences. Some gatherings are weekly, some are daily, all are open to the people who make the house what it is.

What is on
01

Yaya's Corner

Experience Cyprus through the eyes of local Cypriot women as they share seasonal recipes, stories and traditions passed down through generations. Includes wine, beer, water and snacks.

2 to 3 hours · max 12 · €50 pp
02

Breakfast and Swim

Start the day with a refreshing swim followed by a traditional Cypriot breakfast known as Boukoma. Includes pool access and breakfast.

About 2 hours · max 20 · €25 pp
03

Sunrise Yoga

Start the morning with a guided yoga session before enjoying access to the pool. Includes pool access.

About 1 hour · max 12 · €20 pp
04

Water Yoga

A refreshing yoga experience combining movement and mindfulness in and around the pool. Includes pool access.

About 1 hour · max 12 · €20 pp
05

Aperitivo at M House

Inspired by the Italian tradition of gathering before dinner, our daily social ritual. Meet by the pool, enjoy a drink and receive complimentary aperitivo bites while the afternoon turns into evening. Open to guests and visitors, walk-ins welcome.

Daily, 17:00 to 19:00

Unless otherwise stated, all gatherings are available by advance booking only. Aperitivo welcomes walk-ins, though reservations are recommended. Additional seasonal gatherings and experiences will be introduced throughout the year.
